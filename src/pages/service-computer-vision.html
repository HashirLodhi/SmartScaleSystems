Our Process

How We Build Your CV System

01

Data Collection and Annotation

Collect and annotate training images with bounding boxes, polygons, masks, or keypoints per your requirements.

02

Architecture Selection

Choose the right model family (YOLO, ViT, U-Net, etc.) based on accuracy, speed, and deployment requirements.

03

Training and Optimization

Train with data augmentation, transfer learning, and hyperparameter tuning. Optimize for your target hardware.

04

Evaluation and Testing

Validate against mAP, IoU, precision, recall, and edge-case performance benchmarks.

05

Deployment

Deploy as API, edge model, or embedded system with ONNX, TensorRT, or CoreML optimization.
